Bonchon's Story

About Bonchon Philippines

Bonchon, which means “my hometown” in Korean, started as a small store in Busan, South Korea. With its crispy double-fried chicken and signature sauces, it quickly became a global sensation. Since arriving in the Philippines in 2010, Bonchon has redefined fast-casual Korean dining for Filipinos nationwide.

Crafted with Care

Each piece of chicken is hand-battered and cooked to golden perfection. It’s not just food—it’s a crunchy Korean experience.

Hand-Brushed Flavor

Our famous soy garlic and spicy sauces are brushed by hand on every chicken piece for that perfect balance of taste and texture.

K-Style Vibes

Bonchon offers more than chicken. From Bibimbowls to Chapchae, we bring your favorite Korean meals with a Filipino twist.
