Welcome to Bonchon Philippines

Bonchon, meaning “my hometown” in Korean, has been serving delicious Korean-style fried chicken and comfort meals to Filipinos since 2010.

Why Choose Bonchon?

Quality Ingredients

We carefully select the freshest meats and vegetables to ensure each bite delivers unbeatable flavor and crunch.

Signature Korean Taste

Experience the bold and satisfying taste of our double-fried chicken glazed with our one-of-a-kind soy garlic and spicy sauces.

Global Presence

Bonchon brings Korea’s iconic fried chicken to over 300 locations worldwide — including right here in the Philippines since 2010.
